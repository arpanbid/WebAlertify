How to Use WebAlertify

1. Visit the Website

Go to WebAlertify.com.

2. Login or Signup

  • If you're a registered user, log in with your username and password.
  • If you're a new user, sign up with your email ID, username, and password.
  • Note: The username and email must be unique. You cannot reuse already registered usernames or emails.

3. Access the Main Dashboard

Once logged in, you’ll be taken to the main dashboard where you can manage your alerts.

4. Add a New Link for Monitoring

  • Go to the Add Link section.
  • Fill in the form with the following details:
    • Web link: The URL you want to monitor.
    • Link Name: A name for this link.
    • Search Keyword: The keyword you wish to track on the page.
    • Available Keyword: A keyword that’s already present on the page, ensuring the app can access it.
  • Click Check Link to see the status:
    • Positive: The search keyword is available on the website.
    • Negative: The search keyword is not found, but the available keyword is, meaning the app will continue checking the page.
    • Error: The page couldn’t be read. This might happen due to restrictions or CAPTCHA on the site.

5. Add the Link

Once the link is checked, go back and click Add Link. The link will now be visible on your dashboard.

6. Delete a Link

To stop monitoring a link, go to the Delete Link section and remove it from the dashboard.

7. Automated Monitoring

The app will automatically check your links every day at 10 AM IST. If your search keyword is found, you’ll receive an email notification.

8. Log Out

Please remember to Log Out once you have completed your task.
